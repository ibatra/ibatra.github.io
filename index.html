Research and Publications

My research interests include NLP and Machine Learning! The following is a brief summary of paper(s) I've worked on:

  • Learn Languages First and Then Convert: Towards Effective Simplified to Traditional Chinese Conversion

    • Pranav A, S.F. Hui, Ishaan Batra, I-Tsun Cheng and Chiu Yik Hei

    Simplified Chinese to Traditional Chinese Character conversion is a common preprocess-ing step in Chinese NLP. Our proposed method achieved a near-perfect disambiguation accuracy of 99.98%, outperforming previous Chinese Character Conversion approaches by over 5 points in accuracy. Our paper was accepted at NAACL-SRW.
